Who Are We?

The Aggie Club of Engineers is a social, service, and leadership organization strongly rooted in academics. ACE is founded on the idea that engineers should not only develop strongly in academics but also be of strong character. Our aim is to develop our members as successful Aggies and leaders with an emphasis on academic success and character development while also improving Aggieland as a whole by giving back to the community.
